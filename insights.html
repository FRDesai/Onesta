How we make our cheese


Our cheese has never been a secret, it’s something we flex about.


Onesta makes fresh artisanal cheese in its own cheese factory. A team lead by our cheese-master Luigi creates authentic, hygienic cheese for mindblowing pizzas! Our cheese is finely processed & sent to our kitchens. Served hot & melted on your favourites!

Onesta Kitchen Secrets


Serving fresh delights and smiles to you comes with a backstory of creating secret sauces and incredible recipes in the kitchen.


Our chief & chef Armando leads an army of pizza makers who create unique, Onest recipes. With super-secretive sauces, ingredients & procedures, Onesta has been serving Fresh, moody, cheesy recipes in more than 10 outlets.
